Caesar Cipher

Caesar Cipher, also know as caesar's cipher, the shift cipher, caesar's code or caesar shift, is one of the simplest and most widely known encryption teachniques. It is a type of suubstitution cipher in which each letter in the plaintext is replaced by a letter some fixed number of positions down the alphabet.
